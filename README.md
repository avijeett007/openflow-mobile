# OpenFlow Mobile

OpenFlow Mobile is the mobile sibling of OpenFlow desktop: a voice-dictation
**keyboard** for iOS and Android with a companion app. Speak instead of type —
audio is transcribed by your configured speech-to-text provider and optionally
cleaned up by an LLM, then inserted into any text field. Bring your own
endpoints (Groq, OpenAI, Deepgram, OpenRouter, Ollama, or a custom URL); history
and analytics stay on your device. MIT-licensed, OpenFlow branding (violet
`#7C5CFF`).
